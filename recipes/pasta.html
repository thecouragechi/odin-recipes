Pasta

Description

Pasta can come in different forms but my favorite is spaghetti. It is very sweet especially when cooked with fish.

I love it saucy and sweet. So here is how to cook it:

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Meat / Fish
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Crayfish
  • Carrots
  • Green Beans

Steps

  1. Parboil your spaghetti.
  2. Wash and cook your meat/fish to taste.
  3. Fry your meat / fish.
  4. When your spaghetti is parboiled and washed, in a dry pot, put some groundnut oil.
  5. Add diced onions, pepper & crayfish and tomatoes.
  6. Keep stirring until you get a dark red colour.
  7. Add seasonings and bay leaves.(You can decide to add your meat to add more flavour to the meat)
  8. Stir for 3 minutes and add the fish / meat stock.
  9. Add your parboiled spaghetti and stir. If the stock is not enough, add water.
  10. Add salt to taste
  11. Allow to cook till spaghetti is done.
  12. Add diced carrots and green and stir.
  13. Let it cook for 3 minutes and then bring down your pot.

And your delicious pasta is ready to eat.
