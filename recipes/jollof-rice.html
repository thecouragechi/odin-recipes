Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a delicious Nigerian dish. It is made from rice, tomatoes, onions, pepper and seasonings

I usually like my jollof rice very spicy. It is quite easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Meat of your choice
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Bay leaves
  • Crayfish
  • Carrots
  • Green Beans

Steps

  1. Wash your rice properly.
  2. Parboil your rice.
  3. Wash and cook your meat to taste.
  4. Fry your meat.
  5. When you rice is parboiled and washed, in a dry pot, put some groundnut oil.
  6. Add diced onions, pepper & crayfish and tomatoes.
  7. Keep stirring until you get a dark red colour.
  8. Add seasonings and bay leaves.(You can decide to add your meat to add more flavour to the meat)
  9. Stir for 3 minutes and add water.
  10. Add your parboiled rice and stir.
  11. Add salt to taste
  12. Allow to cook till rice is done.
  13. Add diced carrots and green and stir.
  14. Let it cook for 3 minutes and then bring down your pot.

And your sweet jollof rice is ready.
